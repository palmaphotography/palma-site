Hey, I’m Alina — the heart behind Palma Photography. Palma was born in sunny Florida, among palm trees, beaches, and soft golden light. That’s where I first fell in love with photographing the world, and eventually, the people in it. Now I’m based in Seattle, capturing families, couples, motherhood, and portraits with that same love for warmth and connection. For me, photography is about how life feels — the way you reach for each other, laugh, rest, and simply exist together. I’m not here for stiff perfection; I’m here for real, honest moments that feel a little like a movie scene and a lot like you. your Life. Real. Raw. That’s what I’m here to capture.
